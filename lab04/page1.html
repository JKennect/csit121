JPG Format Example

About This Image

For this project, we had to design a cover for a fictional magazine in Adobe Photoshop. The cover image needed to feature a picture that had an effect applied to it. Margins around the cover image were also required. I used a corrupted dog in a haunted forest as the cover story.

Why JPG Format?

Using the JPG format was required for this project. The picture of the dog was taken on an iphone, so it was nescessary to convert from HEIC to JPG. The picture of the forest and the barcode were already JPGs.
